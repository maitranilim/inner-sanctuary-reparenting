# Inner Sanctuary - Reparenting

An interactive and playful website on **Reparenting** - the act of healing the relationship between the adult you are today and the child you once were.

## What is Reparenting?

Reparenting is not just about changing your mind. It's about healing the relationship between the adult you are today and the child you once were. It's an emotional experience that involves replacing the critical inner voice with a nurturing one.

### Key Differences: Reframing vs. Reparenting

**The "Mind" Approach (Cognitive Reframing)**
- Logic-based interpretation
- Focuses on the present "Adult" perspective
- Goal: Objectivity & Fact-checking

**The "Heart" Approach (Reparenting)**
- Feeling-based connection
- Focuses on the "Inner Child's" unmet needs
- Goal: Safety, Validation & Soothing

> "Healing often requires both. You use the 'Adult Mind' to create safety so the 'Inner Child' can feel it."

- Copies wise parent's message and action to clipboard

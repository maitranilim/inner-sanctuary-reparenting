Reparenting is the act of coming home to yourself.

It is not just about changing your mind. It is about healing the relationship between the adult you are today and the child you once were.

Reframing vs. Reparenting

You asked a beautiful question: Is it about changing thoughts, or reassuring the past self? While Cognitive Reframing (changing thoughts) is a powerful tool, Reparenting is distinct because it is an emotional experience.
